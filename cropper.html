Image Cropping FAQ

What image formats can I crop with ImageCropPro?

Our online cropper supports all major image formats including JPG/JPEG, PNG, WebP, and BMP. The cropped image will maintain its original format unless modified during download.

Can I crop images for social media profiles?

Yes! Use our preset aspect ratios to create perfect crops for:

  • Instagram posts (1:1 square)
  • Facebook cover photos (16:9)
  • YouTube thumbnails (16:9)
  • Twitter headers (3:1)

Is my image data secure?

Absolutely. ImageCropPro processes all images client-side in your browser - we never upload your photos to our servers. Your privacy is 100% guaranteed.

Does this work on mobile devices?

Yes, our image cropper is fully responsive and works perfectly on all devices including smartphones and tablets. Touch-friendly interface makes mobile cropping easy.
